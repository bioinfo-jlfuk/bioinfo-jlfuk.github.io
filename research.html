Research

Bioinformatics of Non-Invasive Prenatal Screening (NIPS) is a subject of our research. We have developed a QC criterion that takes into account both fetal fraction and the chromosome representation in deciding whether a sample qualifies for NIPS. We have applied the same principle in developing a method for quantifying the uncertainty of the degree of placental mosaicism of a sample by NIPS.

The research is done in cooperation with the Institute of Molecular Biology and Genomics, JLF UK (Slovakia), the ObGyn Clinic of the University Hospital in Martin (Slovakia) and the Department of Human Genetics, Faculty of Medicine, University of Debrecen (Hungary).
