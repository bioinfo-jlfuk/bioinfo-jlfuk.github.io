Welcome to Bioinformatics Lab of Jessenius Faculty of Medicine, Comenius University

The Laboratory is dedicated to

  • research in bioinformatics of Non-Invasive Prenatal Screening (NIPS)
  • applications of Machine Learning to the analysis of -omics data
  • applied bioinformatics services for analysis of NGS data (DNAseq - variant calling; RNAseq - differential expression, gene fusions; bisulfite sequencing; MTB drug resistance),
  • biomedical data analysis (by statistical methods and/or Machine Learning)
  • consulting and teaching

BioConductor and CLC Genomics Workbench, together with other specialized bioinformatics tools, are used for bioinformatics tasks, followed by data processing and analysis in R.
